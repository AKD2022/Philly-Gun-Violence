In 2022, Guns Caused:
  • 474 Deaths
  • 1788 Victims
  • 516 Homicides
  • 217 Minors To Be Killed

There are many gun violence incidents every year in Philly. These happen in different areas of the city, but according to data, the most violent areas are towards North Philly. In 2022, there were so many gun incidents and even though it was less than 2021, it is still unimaginably higher than before the pandemic. This got me thinking, is the pandemic to blame? Even though there could be many different reasons that these might occur, its important to look at the data we have, because without it, we will never be able to find a way to prevent gun violence
